Why Go To Kushtia

Kushtia Swimming Club

The Kushtia Swimming Club is a community-based swimming facility situated in Mazampur, within the Kushtia Sadar Upazila of Bangladesh's Khulna Division. It is conveniently located near landmarks such as Zia Park, Dar-Us Shefa Private Hospital, and Kushtia Zilla School

Cricket Stadium

The stadium was renamed in 2025 to honor Abrar Fahad, a BUET student who was tragically killed in 2019. The venue has undergone significant renovations from 2020 to 2025 to modernize its facilities

Kushtia Pourashava

Kushtia Municipality is one of the oldest and most significant municipalities in Bangladesh. It was officially established on April 1, 1869, and is located in Kushtia Sadar Upazila, serving as the primary provider of urban administrative and citizen services in Kushtia town.

Historic Kushtia house

In 1895, Rabindranath Tagore involved himself in business. With the assistance of his two nephews, Surendranath and Bolendranath, he established a joint-stock company named Tagore & Company in Shilaidaha. That same year, for better business prospects, Tagore & Company was relocated from Shilaidaha to Kushtia. To manage the company, the poet constructed a two-storied building in the Millpara area of the town.
